How We Met

Our knowing each other was not the regular boy-meet-girl fancy love story, It was rather unconventional.
Adedoyin happened 2b my friend's boyfriend friend. So I remember when he messaged me, I was in my first year in the Uni. I got a text from a strange number and he introduced who he was, and guess I was not too pleased with the whole just sending message out of the blues, so we had a misunderstanding right there and then, 😊
And we didn't speak afterwards for a long time. I later had to reach out, apologize and we became cool friends. Fast forward to 2020, he came to Lagos and I was not too far from where he was at that time, we saw each other for the very first time, 😊, it felt wierdly farmiliar.
And well, the whole love story started.....
-Ifeoluwa

I saw Ife for the first time when I was in Part 3, on my best friend's status. I asked him to link us, but he should first seek permission from her.
I got her number, chatted her up, and she was forming bakan, I just para for her straight and bone.She reached out later and apologized, na so we sha became cool sha. She was so smart, so we related well.
I didn't get to see her in person until 2020, when I came to Lagos, she was staying close by and we just abruptly agreed on seeing, and when we did, we just gelled straight, she was so cool.
-Adedoyin

Our Proposal

As expected, the proposal too was really wierd. 😊 We traveled to Ondo State for his friend's wedding of which he happens to be the best man. I remember how that night went, I was shivering cause rain actually fell on us.
My Bobo tells me to join the girls 4 bridal shower. I was pissed kindof, I was cold and I just needed to crash and I barely knew anyone in the girls' room.
So as good girl, Lol, entered the room, congratulated the bride and greeted her friends. So the guys entered, and I noticed everyone was looking @me, 😊 , I turned to know what's up, then I saw Dee kneeling with a ring in his hand and in my mind I was (like which kind rough play be this, you dey whine me ni?) about to run outside(well, I heard he instructed the door to be shut).
So I stood still, starring at him numb. I said YES! 😀
Well, it was surprising, cause I didn't see it coming at all.
-Ifeoluwa

Hmmm Proposal experience, 😊 Omo, I don dey fear say this girl go japa o, I sha organized with my friends after doing the necessary medical tests, I took her home to meet my parents they liked her, so....I was ready.
On the eve of my padis wedding, I told her to go play with the bride, and I followed from the back, collected ring and just knelt with ring in my hand. Omo, my heart almost ran out of my chest, e no easy to be a man o 😀
She first moved back, looked at everyone strangely, then came to me, hugged me, threatened me, then said YES!
I happy no be small. 😀
-Adedoyin
